Welcome to Philadelphia!

Philadelphia, commonly referred to as Philly, is the most populous city in the U.S. state of Pennsylvania

Philadelphia is a national cultural center, hosting more outdoor sculptures and murals than any other city in the nation.

  • Population: 1.57 million
  • Year Incorporated: 1682
  • City's Location: Eastern side of Pennsylvania
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $57,537

Random Facts about Philadelphia

  1. The city served as the nation's capital until 1800.
  2. Philadelphia was founded in 1682 by William Penn, an English Quaker and advocate of religious freedom.
  3. Philadelphia hosted the First Continental Congress in 1774, preserved the Liberty Bell
