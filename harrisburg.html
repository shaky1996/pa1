Welcome to Harrisburg!

Harrisburg is the capital city of Pennsylvania.

Harrisburg began as a trading post in about 1718. An Englishman named John Harris built the post to trade with the Susquehanna Indians. His son founded a town there in 1785. In 1812 Harrisburg became the capital of Pennsylvania

  • Population: 50,183
  • Year Incorporated: 1791
  • City's Location: Western side of Pennsylvania
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $46,654
